Service of electric cars

The maintenance and care of a car with an electric drive differs from that of cars with a classic internal combustion engine: no spark plugs, no exhaust system, no transmission. And this saves time and money. However, regular servicing at an authorized workshop is important and should not be skipped.


According to the Institute for the Automotive Industry (IFA) at the University of Economics and the Environment (HfWU) in Nürtingen-Geislingen, the repair and maintenance costs of electric cars are about 35% lower than those of classic internal combustion engines. There are also differences in the regular inspection services of cars.

When is it time for an inspection?

According to the manufacturer's instructions, the inspection is recommended after 30,000 km or two years, whichever occurs first. Your Audi e-tron will give you an indication when it's time to visit a workshop.
